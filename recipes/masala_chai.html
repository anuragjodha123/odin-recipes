How to make Masala Chai

Description

Masala Chai is a fragrant and spiced Indian tea, perfect for a refreshing boost. Made with black tea, aromatic spices, and milk, itâ€™s a comforting drink enjoyed across India.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups water
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tsp tea leaves
  • 2-3 crushed cardamom pods
  • 1-inch ginger (grated)
  • 2 tsp sugar (adjust to taste)

Steps

  1. Boil water with ginger and cardamom.
  2. Add tea leaves and simmer for 2 minutes.
  3. Pour in milk and sugar, then bring to a boil.
  4. Strain into cups and serve hot.
